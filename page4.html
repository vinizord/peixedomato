ABOUT US

Following the will of living closer to nature and the desire of simplify the everyday life in order to be happier, we found in the spring of 2011 a true hideaway: a small farm with 40,000 square meters, surrounded by the green of the rainforest, coconut and fruit trees, and near paradisiacal beaches. In the summer of 2014 was born the “Peixe do Mato”, wich could be translated to “jungle fish”. Today our structure has 5 bungalows, camping área and a yoga room. We receive groups for retreats, tourism, travelers and couples who love nature and want to feel the slow life style of hosting. Welcome!
